# DMG Background Placeholder

The DMG installer requires a background image.

## Creating the Background

1. **Size**: 540x380 pixels
2. **Design**: Clean background with Subzilla branding
3. **Format**: PNG with transparency support
4. **Save**: As `dmg-background.png` in this directory

## Temporary Solution

For testing, you can skip the background by commenting out the `background` line in electron-builder.yml
